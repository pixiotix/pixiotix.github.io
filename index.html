About Pixiotix

Pixiotix helps deep-tech businesses start, innovate, deliver and grow.


Deep technology experience

There’s a lot more to software development than typical “full stack” development. Pixiotix has experience across a broad gamut of software development, from working with custom hardware, to embedded systems, operating systems, application software, cloud architecture and of course backend and frontend web development.


Product development

Turning amazing ideas into amazing products takes time, expertise and care. Designing a system that your users will love, that’s fast, reliable, secure and scalable is a massive undertaking and it’s worth planning for up front. Talk to Pixiotix to see how we can help you get prepared for success.


Research and Development, Innovation

Trying to do something that hasn’t been done before? Not sure if it is even possible? Great - Pixiotix is all about innovation! We can help with research, ideation, technical proposals, proof-of-concept through to implementation.


Startups and Scaleups

Building and scaling a startup business is hard & your technology team is key to delivering a great product. Pixiotix helps startups identify people and organisations with the right skills, experience and potential to help you succeed.


Software Engineering

Pixiotix brings a wealth of experience in Software Engineering in real product development environments. We know how to get engineering teams started from scratch, instill good practice and culture, and implement systems and practices that suit your business. Pragmatic Software Engineering allows you to both “get stuff done” and “build great stuff” with a minimum of overhead.
